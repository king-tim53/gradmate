Teaching Practice Module

Your digital classroom assistant. Learn how to create lesson notes, mark attendance, track schemes of work, and manage your finances.

1. Placement Profile Setup

Configure your school details, mentor, and teaching subjects to activate the dashboard.

How to Use:
  • Click the "Setup Placement" button at the top right of the TP page.
  • Enter your School Name (e.g., Kings College) and Assigned Subject.
  • Set your Start & End Dates to activate the countdown timer.
Tip: This initializes your "School Board Card" and Credit Load.

2. Digital Lesson Note Wizard

Create standard Nigerian lesson notes (Ed. 2025 Format) in 4 simple steps.

How to Use:
  • Click "Create New Note" in the Lesson Architect section.
  • Step 1: Enter Topic & Duration. Step 2: Define measurable Objectives.
  • Step 3: Fill the "Teacher Activity" vs "Student Activity" table.
  • Finish: Save it. You can print it or export as PDF for your supervisor.
3. Live Timetable

Manage your teaching schedule dynamically.

  • Click (+) Add Class to schedule a lesson.
  • Select the Day, Start/End Time, and Subject.
  • The dashboard highlights the current active class or shows "Free Period".
4. Allowance Tracker

Track transport costs and stipend spending.

  • Click "Spend" to log an expense (e.g., Bus Fare, Lunch).
  • Click "Add" to log income (e.g., Pocket money, Stipend).
  • View your balance and export your spending history.

Replace the physical register. Create classes, add student names, and mark daily attendance digitally.

Step A: Create Class

Use the dropdown to "Manage Classes". Create "SS 2 Gold" and add student names (e.g., "Musa", "Chidinma").

Step B: Mark Daily
