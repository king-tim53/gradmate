Staff Control Center

Manage 100+ students efficiently. Learn how to use Batch Scheduling, Digital Approvals, and the Shared Repository.

1. Your Unique SPID

This ID is the key that links students to your dashboard. It combines your Title, Initials, and University.

How to Use:
  • Locate your SPID at the top right of the Overview page.
  • Click the Copy Icon to share it with your students via WhatsApp or Email.
  • Once a student enters this ID, they appear in your list automatically (if Auto-Accept is ON).
Example: SPID-DR-2356177-FA-OAU

3. Batch Scheduling Tool

Stop messaging students one by one. Use the Matric Number Range tool to summon specific groups.

  • Go to the Group Schedule tab.
  • Enter Start Matric (e.g., ...005) and End Matric (e.g., ...015).
  • Set the Date & Time.
  • Click Broadcast. Only those 10 students will get the invite.
Efficiency Hack

Great for dividing 100 students into 5 groups of 20 for weekly checks.

4. Broadcast System

Send a priority notification to ALL students at once.

PREVIEW "I will not be available on Monday. Meetings shifted."

Located at the top of the Overview page. Ideal for sudden schedule changes.

5. Shared Repository

Upload once, distribute to everyone.

  • Navigate to Shared Repository in the sidebar.
  • Upload files like "Chapter 1 Template.docx" or "APA Guide.pdf".
  • It instantly appears in the Resources folder of every student linked to you.
6. Gatekeeper Settings
Auto-Accept ON
Students join your list immediately after entering SPID.
Auto-Accept OFF
Students go to "Pending Requests" card. You must approve each one.
