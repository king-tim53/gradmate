Project Defense Module

Master your final year research with this step-by-step guide. Learn how to track progress, simulate defenses, and generate citations.

1. Project Identity Setup

The control center for your research. Configure your topic, supervisor, and deadlines here.

How to Use:
  • Click the "Project Setup" button at the top right.
  • Enter your Topic, Supervisor Name, and Defense Date.
  • Define your Sub-topics for Chapter 1 & 2 for better tracking.
Example: Setting a Defense Date automatically activates the "Days Left" countdown.

2. Thesis Roadmap

A neon-styled visual progress bar that tracks your journey from Chapter 1 to 5.

How to Use:
  • Click on a Chapter Number (1-5) when you complete it.
  • The neon pipeline will fill up, and your percentage (e.g., 20%, 40%) will update.
  • The status badge changes phases (e.g., "Literature Review" to "Methodology").
Note: Progress is saved automatically. If you leave and come back, it remembers you are in Ch. 3.
3. Anti-Loss Vault

Cloud storage simulation to prevent file loss.

  • Click any Chapter Card.
  • Use the popup to upload your .docx or .pdf file.
  • The card turns green (Synced) once uploaded.
4. Consultation Log

Keep receipts of what your supervisor said.

  • Click the (+) button.
  • Log the Feedback (e.g., "Change the font size").
  • Log Next Steps. It creates a searchable history.
5. Correction Tracker

A To-Do list specifically for thesis corrections.

  • Type a task (e.g., "Fix margins").
  • Select Chapter and toggle High Priority if urgent.
  • Check the box to strike it through when done.

6. AI Defense Simulator

This is your secret weapon. It contains a bank of 25+ Standard Nigerian University Defense Questions.

Step A: Simulate

Click "Next Question" to get a random tough question like "What is the gap in knowledge?"

Step B: Use Hints

Stuck? Click "Reveal Hint". It tells you exactly what the examiner wants to hear.
