Dashboard & Overview

Your central command center. Learn how to manage your identity, track global progress, and access specific academic modules.

1. Smart Profile & Identity

Your generated ID (e.g., S-2025-001) tells the system which modules you have access to.

How to Modify Access:
  • Locate your Profile Card at the top of the dashboard.
  • Click the small "Change" button next to your ID.
  • Select/Deselect checkboxes (SIWES, Project, Teaching Practice) to update your track.
Tip: An ID starting with "S-P" means you have both SIWES and Project active.

2. Module Command Center

The gateway to your specific tasks. Each card represents a distinct academic phase.

How to Use:
  • View the "Quick Status" badge (e.g., "Active", "Week 1") on the card.
  • Click the "Enter" button to jump to that module's full page.
  • If a module is locked, use the Profile "Change" button to enable it.
Note: The Dashboard gives a summary; the Module page handles the work.
3. Progress Analytics

Visual indicators of how close you are to completion.

  • SIWES %: Increases as you fill your logbook weeks.
  • Project %: Increases as you complete chapters (1-5).
  • TP %: Tracks your lesson notes and weeks covered.
4. Master Timeline

One calendar to rule them all. Consolidates dates from all modules.

  • Click the "Add Task" button.
  • Select a category (Project or SIWES).
  • The system sorts them automatically by date (Next up appears first).
5. Naija Tips

Localized advice for Nigerian students.

  • Updates every time you refresh the dashboard.
  • Provides practical advice like "Prepare 3 hard copies" or "Buy snacks for your supervisor".

6. Academic Knowledge Hub

An intelligent FAQ system built right into your dashboard. Stop asking peers rumors; get facts about ITF, Defense, and Plagiarism.

Step A: Search

Type keywords like "ITF" or "Defense" in the search bar. The list filters instantly.
