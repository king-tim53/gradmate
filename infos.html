SIWES Companion

Your complete industrial training toolkit. Manage logbooks, track supervisor visits, and auto-generate reports.

1. Placement Profile

Set up your industrial training details to start the countdown timer and progress tracking.

How to Use:
  • Click "Setup Placement" (top right).
  • Input your Company Name, Job Role, and Dates.
  • The system automatically calculates your total weeks (e.g., 24 Weeks).
Tip: Ensure dates match your official SCAF letter.

2. Daily Logbook System

A digital replacement for your physical logbook. Never forget what you did on Monday.

How to Use:
  • Click "New Entry".
  • Describe work done (Technical) and Lessons Learned (Soft Skills).
  • Use the Search Bar to find specific entries later (e.g., search "Networking").
Goal: Keeps your "Logbook Health" at 100%.
3. Supervisor Tracker

Record official school visitations.

  • Click "Log New Visit" when your lecturer arrives.
  • The status icon turns Green.
  • View "Visitation History" to prove attendance.
4. Industry Toolbag

Showcase the software & tools you mastered.

  • Click "Add Skill".
  • Add tools like AutoCAD, Python, VS Code.
  • Builds a competency profile for your CV.
5. Document Vault

Safe storage for ITF forms.

  • Upload SCAF Form, Form 8, or Acceptance Letter.
  • Access them anywhere (e.g., at the cyber cafe).
  • Prevent losing physical papers.

6. Automatic Technical Report Builder

Stop stressing about writing your final report from scratch. GradMate uses your Daily Logs to generate a structured 6-month Technical Report outline automatically.
